How to Play

A two-player memory game: flip fruit tiles, find matching pairs, and race for the highest score.

  1. Players take turns opening two tiles.
  2. Two matching fruits score 30 points and stay open.
  3. A missed pair flips back, then the turn changes.
  4. Find all 18 pairs to see the winner.
